Silicon Nights: About Us

Our Story...

  Silicon Nights was founded one dark stormy night (actually it was a bright clear morning) barely eighteen years into the 21st century. The Founding Knights (David, Baldemar, Saif, Rafael and Brian) formed a brotherhood, and sought to create the Silicon Knights. Shortly thereafter, the name was changed due to an already existing company of the same name. The Founding Knights -- Nights? -- joined forces for one sole purpose: To create a cool little website and search for recipes based on ingredients a user may have, and also to get an A in a Software Engineering class. This is that website!
